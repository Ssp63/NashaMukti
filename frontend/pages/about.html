About Us

Learn about the Nasha Mukti Portal and our mission

Our Mission

The Nasha Mukti Portal by Sangli Zilla Parishad is dedicated to tackling substance abuse and addiction through education, prevention, support, and treatment. We aim to create a healthier and addiction-free community by providing resources, guidance, and a platform for those affected by addiction, their families, and healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

We envision a society where individuals have access to the knowledge, tools, and support they need to prevent, treat, and recover from addiction. By breaking the stigma surrounding addiction and providing accessible resources, we strive to transform lives and communities.

Our History

The Nasha Mukti Portal was established in 2023 by the Sangli Zilla Parishad in response to the growing concerns about substance abuse in the region. Recognizing the need for a comprehensive approach to tackle addiction, the Zilla Parishad launched this initiative to provide a centralized platform for information, support, and resources.

Since its inception, the portal has evolved to serve thousands of individuals, families, and professionals, becoming a vital resource in the fight against addiction in Sangli district and beyond.
