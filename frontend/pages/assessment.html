Self-Assessment

Take a confidential assessment to understand your relationship with substances

Why Take an Assessment?

Understanding if you have an addiction problem is the first step toward recovery. Our assessments are designed to help you evaluate your substance use patterns and determine if professional help might be needed.

Completely Confidential

Your responses are anonymous and private

Quick & Easy

Takes only 5-10 minutes to complete

Scientifically Validated

Based on established clinical assessments
